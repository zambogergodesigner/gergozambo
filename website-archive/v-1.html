Eötvös Loránd University

Brand Design, Print Design, Ux Research

I worked as a graphic designer for the Eötvös Loránd University where I created graphics, print materials, and full identities for international conferences, media campaings, and organisations. I also did an in-depth heuristic website and Ux research when the time came for the redesign of our website.

The source code of this website is under 10 kilobytes. I symphatized with the 10k Apart competition and designed my site according to this page weight limit. This makes it around 225 times smaller than an avarage website as of 2016 and around 102 times smaller than the standard page size reference. The site has been developed using mobile-ﬁrst css, responsive typography techniques, the good old posh method, the html validates ﬂawlessly and the site is accessible to a wide range of users. The experience differs from device to device, but with progressive enhancement in mind, each device gets the needed assets for the best possible experience. I am an advocate of inclusive web design, which is wired into the leading web design community.
