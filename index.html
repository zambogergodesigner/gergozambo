Literary Magazine

Website Design

I was a brand and user experience consultant during the website design of Literary Magazine. My role was to make the brand come alive within the online space and strengthen the brand’s position and recognition. Literary Magazine’s website is one of the most usable and recognizable in this market space.

The source code of this website is under 10 kilobytes. I symphatized with the 10k Apart competition and designed my site according to this page weight limit. This makes it around 250 times smaller than an avarage website as of 2023 and around 102 times smaller than the standard page size reference. The site has been developed using mobile-ﬁrst css, responsive typography techniques, the posh method, the html validates ﬂawlessly, and the site is accessible to a wide range of users.
